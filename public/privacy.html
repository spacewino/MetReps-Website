Privacy Policy for MetReps

Last Updated: July 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MetReps ("we", "our", "us") handles your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy. MetReps is designed from the ground up as a privacy-first, offline-first application.

1. No Collection of Personal Information

MetReps does not require you to create an account, log in, or register to use the App. We do not collect, store, or transmit any Personally Identifiable Information (PII), such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical location.

2. On-Device Local Data Storage

All data related to your workouts, exercises, programs, lifting statistics, historical logs, and body metrics are stored strictly on your local device.

  • This data is saved within your device’s secure local storage (such as Web Storage, SQLite, or local application directories).
  • We have no access to this data. Your training history and progress are yours alone. If you clear your App data, delete the App, or factory-reset your device, your logged training data will be permanently deleted as we do not keep remote backups.

3. Camera and Video Permissions (If Applicable)

If the App utilizes camera permissions (for local, real-time trajectory velocity tracking or workout recording):

  • Zero Uploads: No video footage, stream, or images are ever transmitted to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.
  • On-Device Only: Any video or camera feed is processed entirely in real-time, on-device, and on your local hardware. No video recordings are saved or stored unless explicitly exported by you to your device's photo library.

4. Third-Party Services

MetReps is fully functional offline and does not integrate third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks that collect or share your personal data.

5. Children's Privacy

Because MetReps does not collect, process, or transmit any data of any kind (including from children), we do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe we have somehow obtained data, please contact us immediately, though we confirm no such data is technically stored by us.

6. Security

Your data is as secure as your physical device. Because all of your information is stored on-device, we recommend protecting your smartphone or tablet with secure passcodes, biometric authentication, and regular device-level backups to protect your local workout logs.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our App's functionality or to comply with legal requirements. Any updates will be posted directly within this document, with the updated "Last Updated" date at the top.
